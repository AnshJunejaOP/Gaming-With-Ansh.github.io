An unconventional battle royale game

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout is a paid online battle royale game that you can download on your Windows device. The survival game pits you against 59 players, places you in random teams, throws in several obstacles, and eliminates all players until there’s only one remaining. It’s an unconventional battle royale game where players look like jelly beans and compete in a bright pink world. The party game features vibrant graphics and sound and is suitable for both children and adults. 

What is Fall Guys: The Ultimate Knockout?

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout is a fun and entertaining game created and published by Mediatonic and Developer Digital, respectively. The battle royale game features multiple rounds with 60 players competing to reach the finish line first. Each level is randomly generated and tries to eliminate as many as 30 players in one go. It does this by putting the jelly beans through several obstacle courses.

How can you play Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout?

Once you download Fall Guys on your computer, you can start playing instantly by joining a match. The game features more than a dozen randomly-generated rounds that last a maximum of 3 minutes. The full battle royale game doesn’t take up more than 25 minutes. Losses and wins don’t last very long, and the more you play, the better your chances of becoming the last player standing. 

Each round or level sees a large number of jelly beans jumping, running, diving, and rolling through an obstacle course. While the game offers races on different levels, each race has its obstacle, ranging from slippery ramps to vanishing tiles to spinning beams to large fruits falling from the sky. The challenges are designed to knock down players so that they can’t make it to the finish line on time. 

Apart from races, Fall Guys PC also tests endurance in some rounds. For instance, the game offers a challenge where players stand on platforms while the walls begin to move inwards. Players must force themselves through narrow lanes and use small gaps to avoid falling. You can also dive through openings and wriggle your way out of a bottleneck to keep things exciting. 

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout also offers levels where players are divided into different teams. Each team’s purpose to achieve a designated goal while making sure the other team loses. In team mode, you’re able to easily sabotage other players. However, if you’re playing with a team of strangers instead of your friends, you may end up losing, considering you have no way to talk to them. 

What can players expect from the game’s graphics?

When you download the game Fall Guys on your PC, you should expect to land in a vivid world with bright shades of yellow, pink, and red. Offering a kaleidoscope of colors, the game features jelly beans running around in a chaotic mess of fruits, obstacles, and sweets. In other words, the game looks like a cartoon version of Takeshi’s Castle game show. 

How can players control their characters?

When you play Fall Guys on your Windows PC, you only need your keyboard to control your avatar. The game requires players to jump, run, dive, grab, and hold on for dear life. To manage moving forward, backward, left, or right, all you need are the WASD keys. Apart from them, you can use the SpaceBar, Shift key, and the F key to jump, dive and grab. The Tab key reveals the names of all players. 

Does Fall Guys offer any customization options?

The game’s jelly bean characters look like minions and have big expressive eyes, legs, and arms. With Fall Guys free customization options, players can experiment extensively with the look of their avatar. Players can wear different costumes, change colors, wear headdresses, embellish with belts, and change shoes. Once the game begins, all 60 players line up in colorful outfits customized to no end!

Does the game feature any in-game currency?

When you download Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout, you start without many customization options. This is because outfits, costumes, and accessories are all items that you need to unlock as you begin playing. Experience points, gold coins, and Kudoz, the game’s currency, are awarded to players as they cross different rounds and can be used to purchase items and pass levels. 

Is Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout easy to play?

While Fall Guys PC download seems like a fun and easy game to play, it can get quite challenging. Each round comes with its own set of challenges, which means that you could be great at one level and instantly lose in another. The idea is to get better at controlling your character so that even if you find yourself in difficult scenarios, you’re able to jump, run, dive, grab and defy physics laws. 

Are there any drawbacks?

Even though Fall Guys: Ultimate can be played with a total of 60 players, you can only enter the game as a party of 4. Any player beyond the 4 member party may or may not end up in the same online tournament. It would have been great if the game could offer custom matches so that players could invite all their family members and friends to get in on the action of one knockout game. 

Does the game have any specific system requirements?

Fall Guys system requirements for Windows 10 include a 64-bit operating system, 8 GB RAM, Intel iCore i5 processor, NVIDIA GTX 660, and 2 GB free disk space. 

Are there any Fall Guys alternatives?

Among Us is one of the most prominent alternatives to Fall Guys. Minecraft and Gang Beasts are two fun party games that you might want to play during social gatherings. In case you’re looking for a more serious battle game, you may want to check out Fortnite Battle Royale.

Should I download Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout?

If you’re looking for a fun and entertaining survival game, you should download Fall Guys; Ultimate Knockout. It’s a battle royale game that can entice adults and kids alike and features jelly bean characters that compete against each other in different obstacle courses. Each round lasts a few minutes and offers dramatic wins, heart-pumping acrobatics, and sad losses. The game relies on an online community and its addictive loop-style gameplay.
